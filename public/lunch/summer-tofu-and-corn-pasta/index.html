Summer Tofu and Corn Pasta

Very tasty and savory.

INGRIDIENT

DIRECTION

Step: 1

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta, and cook for 13 to 15 minutes or until al dente; drain.

Step: 2

Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat, and stir in the tofu. Mix in honey and Dijon mustard. Season with dry mustard, 1 teaspoon

Step: 3

® Seasoning, kosher salt, and pepper. Cook and stir until tofu is thoroughly coated.

Step: 4

Mix corn into the saucepan. Season with remaining Old Bay® Seasoning and nutritional yeast. Cook until heated through. Allow to cool about 5 minutes before serving over the cooked pasta.

NUTRITION FACT

Per Serving: 291 calories; protein 14.2g; carbohydrates 48.6g; fat 5.3g; cholesterol 41.5mg; sodium 466mg.
