This soup is very filling and healthy. If possible use fresh vegetables and herbs from the garden. If you like, you can replace the kidney beans with cannelini beans. 

{{< boldheading >}}

{{< checkbox "4 cups vegetable stock" >}}
{{< checkbox "2 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 large potato, cubed" >}}
{{< checkbox "1  onion, chopped" >}}
{{< checkbox "2 stalks celery, chopped" >}}
{{< checkbox "2  carrots, chopped" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 large head cabbage, finely chopped" >}}
{{< checkbox "2 tablespoons Italian seasoning" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans" >}}
{{< checkbox "3 cups fresh corn kernels" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 large zucchini, sliced" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 cup uncooked orzo pasta" >}}
{{< checkbox "salt and pepper to taste" >}}


{{< direction >}}

**Step: 1**

In a large soup pot combine the vegetable stock, the undrained  tomatoes, potato, onion, celery, carrot, cabbage and Italian seasoning. Bring to a boil and reduce heat. Simmer for about 15 minutes.{{< span >}}

**Step: 2**

Stir in the beans, corn, zucchini and pasta; simmer for 10 to 15 more minutes until the vegetables are tender. Season with salt and pepper.{{< span >}}
