In a hurry?  Make this fruit salad in about 10 minutes.  You can add or subtract different fruit according to your taste and what is in season. 

{{< boldheading >}}

{{< checkbox "1 pint strawberries - cleaned, hulled and sliced" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 pound seedless grapes, halved" >}}
{{< checkbox "3  kiwis, peeled and sliced" >}}
{{< checkbox "3  bananas, sliced" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 (21 ounce) can peach pie filling" >}}


{{< direction >}}

**Step: 1**

In a large bowl, combine the strawberries, grapes, kiwis, and bananas. Gently mix in peaches.  Chill for 1 hour before serving.{{< span >}}
