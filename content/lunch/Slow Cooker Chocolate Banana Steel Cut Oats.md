Just another way for oatmeal to be enjoyed! Makes plenty of leftovers to freeze for later. 

{{< boldheading >}}

{{< checkbox "cooking spray" >}}
{{< checkbox "10 cups water" >}}
{{< checkbox "2 cups steel-cut oats" >}}
{{< checkbox "2 pounds ripe bananas, mashed" >}}
{{< checkbox "½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder" >}}
{{< checkbox "⅓ cup granular no-calorie sucralose sweetener (such as Splenda®)  (Optional)" >}}


{{< direction >}}

**Step: 1**

Lightly spray a 5-quart or larger slow cooker crock with cooking spray.{{< span >}}

**Step: 2**

Mix water, oats, mashed bananas, cocoa powder, and sweetener in prepared slow cooker.{{< span >}}

**Step: 3**

Cook on Low for 6 hours.{{< span >}}
