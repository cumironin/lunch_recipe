Only four ingredients are needed to make this hearty Italian inspired chicken dinner. Serve with rice and a tossed salad. 

{{< boldheading >}}

{{< checkbox "4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 (4 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 (15 ounce) can cannellini beans, rinsed and drained" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 (26.5 ounce) can roasted garlic flavored spaghetti sauce" >}}


{{< direction >}}

**Step: 1**

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Coat a 9x13 inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.{{< span >}}

**Step: 2**

Place the chicken breasts in the baking dish, and sprinkle olives over the top. Pour the beans over the chicken, then pour the spaghetti sauce over everything.{{< span >}}

**Step: 3**

Cover, and bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven. Remove the cover, and continue baking for 10 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink inside.{{< span >}}
