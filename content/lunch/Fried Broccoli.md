This is a recipe I came up with when I was on the South Beach Diet. It is a very healthy recipe and my husband asks for it almost all the time. 

{{< boldheading >}}

{{< checkbox "1 (16 ounce) package frozen broccoli, thawed" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 tablespoon olive oil" >}}
{{< checkbox "½ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes" >}}
{{< checkbox "salt, to taste" >}}


{{< direction >}}

**Step: 1**

Rinse and pat dry the broccoli.{{< span >}}

**Step: 2**

Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat, add the crushed red pepper and heat 1 minute. Cook and stir the broccoli in the skillet until it begins to get crispy, 5 to 7 minutes. Season with salt to serve.{{< span >}}
