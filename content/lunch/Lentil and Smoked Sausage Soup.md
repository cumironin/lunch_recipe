A hearty soup, packed with lentils, smoked vegetarian sausage, onion, cabbage, and tomatoes. 

{{< boldheading >}}

{{< checkbox "2 tablespoons olive oil" >}}
{{< checkbox "7 ounces dry lentils" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 small onion, chopped" >}}
{{< checkbox "¾ cup finely shredded green cabbage" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 clove garlic, crushed" >}}
{{< checkbox "2  vegetarian smoked sausages, diced" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 (16 ounce) can diced tomatoes" >}}
{{< checkbox "2 cubes  vegetable bouillon" >}}
{{< checkbox "4 cups water" >}}
{{< checkbox "1  bay leaf" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 teaspoon thyme leaves" >}}
{{< checkbox "½ teaspoon cayenne pepper" >}}
{{< checkbox "salt and pepper to taste" >}}


{{< direction >}}

**Step: 1**

Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in lentils, onion, cabbage, and garlic; cook until tender. Stir in sausage and tomatoes. Crumble vegetable cubes over mixture and pour in water. Stir in bay leaf, thyme, and cayenne pepper. Bring to a boil; cover, and simmer until lentils are tender, about 30 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.{{< span >}}
