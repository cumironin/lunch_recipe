A Southern salad that mixes the sweet juices of pineapple with the spicy tang of Cajun pepper. 

{{< boldheading >}}

{{< checkbox "¼ cup mayonnaise" >}}
{{< checkbox "⅛ cup prepared mustard" >}}
{{< checkbox "4 teaspoons sweet relish" >}}
{{< checkbox "2 (16 ounce) cans pineapple chunks" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 teaspoon Cajun pepper" >}}
{{< checkbox "2 pounds iceberg lettuce, shredded" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 cup bay leaves" >}}


{{< direction >}}

**Step: 1**

In a small mixing bowl, stir together mayonnaise, mustard, and relish. Mix well until it becomes a nice pastel color.{{< span >}}

**Step: 2**

In a separate bowl, stir together pineapple and Cajun pepper.{{< span >}}

**Step: 3**

Place shredded lettuce into a large bowl. Pour pineapple mixture on top of the lettuce. Place bay leaves as a garnish around the edge of the bowl. Drizzle dressing lightly over the salad.{{< span >}}
