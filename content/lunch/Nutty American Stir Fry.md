Simple healthy stir-fry recipe. Great vegetarian dish because it has protein. It is low fat and it has healthy spices too. 

{{< boldheading >}}

{{< checkbox "2 cups water" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 cup white rice" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 tablespoon sesame oil" >}}
{{< checkbox "5 cups chopped collard greens" >}}
{{< checkbox "⅛ teaspoon minced garlic" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 pinch red pepper flakes, or to taste" >}}
{{< checkbox "⅓ cup almonds" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 teaspoon lemon juice, or as needed" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 teaspoon soy sauce" >}}
{{< checkbox "¼ teaspoon chopped fresh ginger" >}}


{{< direction >}}

**Step: 1**

Bring water and rice to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender and the liquid has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.{{< span >}}

**Step: 2**

Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir{{< span >}}

**Step: 3**

, garlic, and red pepper flakes in the hot oil until collard greens are wilted and tender, about 10 minutes.{{< span >}}

**Step: 4**

Mix almonds, lemon juice, soy sauce, and ginger into collard green mixture; simmer until liquid evaporates, about 5 minutes. Serve with rice on the side.{{< span >}}
