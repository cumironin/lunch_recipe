{{< boldheading >}}

{{< checkbox "½ cup plain yogurt" >}}
{{< checkbox "½ cup crushed pineapple in juice" >}}
{{< checkbox "3 large carrots, shredded" >}}
{{< checkbox "¼ cup raisins, or more to taste" >}}


{{< direction >}}

**Step: 1**
