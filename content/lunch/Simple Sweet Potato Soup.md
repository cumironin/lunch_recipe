This delicious soup has only 6 ingredients.  It was given to me by my boyfriend's mom who adapted it from another recipe.  It can be as thick or thin as you like, and I like it quite thick.  To dress it up when serving, drizzle some cream on top and float a lime twist or slice of lime on top.  Delicious hot or cold. 

{{< boldheading >}}

{{< checkbox "6 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and chopped" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger" >}}
{{< checkbox "2 cups chicken or vegetable stock" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 cup water, or as needed" >}}
{{< checkbox "1  lime, juiced" >}}
{{< checkbox "2 cups milk or cream, or as needed" >}}


{{< direction >}}

**Step: 1**

Place potatoes and ginger in large saucepan. Pour in chicken stock and water, and bring to a boil over high heat.  Reduce heat to medium low, cover, and simmer until the potatoes have softened, about 30 minutes.{{< span >}}

**Step: 2**

Puree potatoes and lime juice in a food processor, or with a hand blender until smooth.  Return soup to saucepan and stir in milk or cream as desired; heat until warmed through.{{< span >}}
