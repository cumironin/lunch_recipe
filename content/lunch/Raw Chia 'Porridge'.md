Easy, affordable raw breakfast treat. Top with your favorite fresh fruit! 

{{< boldheading >}}

{{< checkbox "¼ cup chia seeds" >}}
{{< checkbox "1  banana" >}}
{{< checkbox "2  dates, pitted" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 cup almond milk" >}}
{{< checkbox "¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon" >}}
{{< checkbox "salt to taste" >}}
{{< checkbox "¼ cup fresh blueberries, or more to taste" >}}


{{< direction >}}

**Step: 1**

Place chia seeds in a bowl.{{< span >}}

**Step: 2**

Layer banana and dates in a blender; add almond milk, cinnamon, and salt. Blend mixture until smooth and pour over chia seeds, stirring well. Let mixture sit until thickened, at least 15 minutes.{{< span >}}

**Step: 3**

Stir chia 'porridge' and top with blueberries.{{< span >}}
