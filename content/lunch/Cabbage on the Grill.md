This is a quick way to cook cabbage on the grill. It gets really sweet when cooked this way. 

{{< boldheading >}}

{{< checkbox "1 large head cabbage" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 ½ teaspoons garlic powder, or to taste" >}}
{{< checkbox "salt and pepper to taste" >}}


{{< direction >}}

**Step: 1**

Preheat grill for medium heat.{{< span >}}

**Step: 2**

Cut the cabbage into 8 wedges, and remove the core. Place all the wedges on a piece of aluminum foil large enough to wrap the cabbage. Season to taste with garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Seal cabbage in the foil.{{< span >}}

**Step: 3**

Grill for 30 to 40 minutes on the preheated grill, until tender.{{< span >}}
