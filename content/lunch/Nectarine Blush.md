I love making this drink in the summer with fresh nectarines. It is also really good made with fresh peaches. The buttermilk gives it a fresh and tangy flavor while the brown sugar disguises the fact you are using buttermilk! 

{{< boldheading >}}

{{< checkbox "2  nectarines, pitted and chopped" >}}
{{< checkbox "2 tablespoons brown sugar" >}}
{{< checkbox "1 cup buttermilk" >}}


{{< direction >}}

**Step: 1**

Place the nectarines, brown sugar and buttermilk in the bowl of a blender and blend until smooth. Serve immediately.{{< span >}}
